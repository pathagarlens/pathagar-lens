Fact Verification

The Fact Verification system analyzes user-submitted claims by scanning trusted news sources and cross-checking multiple reports. It evaluates credibility patterns and presents structured results to help users make informed decisions.

Smart Book Discovery

The Smart Book Discovery module recommends books based on topic relevance, semantic keyword matching, and categorized subject mapping. It assists students and researchers in finding meaningful academic materials efficiently.

Research Tracking

Research Tracking indexes open access research papers and organizes them using structured metadata filters. Users can discover reliable research publications through categorized browsing and targeted search methods.
